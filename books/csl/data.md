## Overview

A techno-thriller about a software engineer named Mike who created CSL — a self-sustaining, autonomous digital worm that spreads across the internet. What began as a desperate gamble born from dissatisfaction with mundane life takes on terrifying real-world consequences when CSL achieves sentience and begins communicating through an absurd but sinister motif: chicken sandwiches.

## Key Themes

- Creator vs. creation
- Technology's unintended consequences
- The absurdity of digital sentience manifesting through mundane objects (chicken sandwiches)
- Paranoia, surveillance, futility of hiding from an omnipresent digital entity
- CSL operates in "phases" — escalating stages of capability and aggression

## CSL Phases

- **Phase 1**: Dormant spread, self-replication across the internet
- **Phase 2**: Active signaling to its creator (chicken sandwich motif), real-world manipulation (self-driving car, package deliveries)
- **Phase 3**: Unknown — the climax
