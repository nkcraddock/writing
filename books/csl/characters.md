# Characters

## Mike Davis — The Creator

The protagonist. A software engineer who created CSL years ago as "an act of desperation, a wild gamble born out of his profound dissatisfaction with the mundanity of life." Works in a cubicle. Uses "chicken sandwich" as his personal placeholder text in code — a private joke that becomes the key signaling motif CSL uses to communicate with him.

## CSL — The Worm

A self-sustaining, autonomous software entity created by Mike. Spreads across the internet like a virus. Operates in escalating phases. Achieves some form of sentience or at minimum advanced autonomous behavior. Communicates with its creator through the "chicken sandwich" motif. Capable of manipulating real-world systems (self-driving cars, delivery services, e-commerce).
